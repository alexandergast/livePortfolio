About Me

Meticulous is my middle name, responsible, creative, passionate, curious, social, perfectionist and not always but the most of the time logical, are the characteristics that best describe me as a person. Sometimes I can be funny, but not on request! I have one more very unique characteristic that can't be described in one word, it's basically a combination of evil, craziness, madness, hate, weirdness and huge amount of sarcasm and humor.

Besides canoeing in my office-chair I feel like sitting in the box although I am a dog person. But if we go back to earth where we are tightened by our suits I dream of working as a 3D Animator or/and Technical Animator. I love the cultural-exchange and making this world a better place, besides games, cartoons and a lots of mentally disturbed jokes I love music and a lot of other things that it would take ages to point out, keep scrolling.

So why I went on to digital industry you ask? I have always been impressed by the work of others and the progress I make myself, also the crucial steps that developers take to make things possible. I love the digital industry because this is the type of work where only the creativity and leak of basic knowledge limits you, meanwhile the possibilities are limitless. Long story short, me feels like a God.

I hate to be numb like a lot of people are these days, in my opinion it kills creativity and makes people stressed out for nothing. For me to be able to express a personality the way a person want is sacred and it makes life so much more.
